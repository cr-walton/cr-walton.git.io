ABOUT ME

I am a software developer based in Cardiff, with an interest in backend development. So far I have relished the opportunity to work with databases and PHP. I will graduate from iO Academy in October 2022.

Prior to my training, I was completing a biology degree and have a deep interest in conservation and the environment. My strengths include communication skills and problem-solving.

Outside of work I enjoy reading science fiction novels, playing Dungeons & Dragons with a group of close friends, hiking around South Wales and watching rugby. I also have an minor obsession with cooking and love discovering new restaurants.
